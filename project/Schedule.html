Schedule of The Chinese Soccer Team

The draw of the round of 12 has ended, and the schedule of the round of 12 has been confirmed. The top 12 were divided into two groups, with the top two advancing directly and the third competing for a place in the inter-continental play-off. Apart from host Qatar, the Region has 4.5 qualifying places for the 2022 World Cup (4 direct qualifying places +1 cross-continent play-off place).

Round 1: September 2, China (away) vs Australia
Round 2: September 7th, China (home) vs Japan
Round 3: Oct 7, China (home) vs Vietnam
Round 4: Oct 12, China (away) vs Saudi Arabia
Round 5: Nov 11, China (home) vs Oman
Round 6: Nov 16, China (home) vs Australia
Round 7: Jan 27, 2022, China (away) vs Japan
Round 8: Feb 1, 2022 (Chinese New Year's Day), China (away) vs Vietnam
Round 9: March 24, 2022, China (home) vs Saudi Arabia
Round 10:29 March 2022, China (away) vs Oman
